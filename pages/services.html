Claims Processing

Fast and reliable processing of claims with accredited facilities.

Claims Process

For direct filing, submit your claim within 60 calendar days after discharge. For reimbursement, submit your claim within 120 calendar days.

Required Documents

  • PhilHealth Benefit Eligibility Form
  • Member Data Record
  • Medical certificate or clinical abstracts
  • Official receipts for hospital expenses
Download claims forms

Online Registration

Easy online application for new members through the Member Portal.

Registration Steps

  1. Visit the PhilHealth Member Portal
  2. Select "Register as New Member"
  3. Fill out the electronic Personal Record form
  4. Upload required documents
  5. Submit your application
  6. Receive your PhilHealth ID number via email
